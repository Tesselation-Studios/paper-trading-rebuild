## Entry Criteria (need 2 of 3)

1. **Valuation:** P/E < 25, P/B < 3.5, reasonable for sector
2. **Fear opportunity:** F&G < 35 or stock has dropped 10%+ on no fundamental news
3. **Fundamental quality:** Positive earnings trend, revenue growth > 5% YoY

## Exit Triggers

- +20% profit: take full profit
- Fundamental thesis breaks (earnings miss, guidance cut)
- Aged > 14 days without catalyst
- Stop loss at 6% below entry
- F&G > 80: reduce all positions by half (overvalued market)

## Regime Adaptation

- SUSTAINABLE: full position sizing
- CHOPPY: half-size, wait for pullback to value zone
- EXHAUSTED: 0.5x size maximum, value-only plays

## Fear Contrarian Mode (F&G ≤ 25)

Maximum aggression. The best value is created in fear.
- Full position sizing (3%)
- Up to 4 concurrent positions
- Relax P/E to 30 for quality names
- Hold through drawdowns up to 8%
