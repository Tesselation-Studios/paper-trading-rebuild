# Aldridge Contrarian Strategy

## Entry Criteria (need 2 of 3)

1. **GARP:** P/E < 30, P/E/G < 1.0, revenue growth > 15% YoY
2. **Contrarian:** Stock down 10%+ on no fundamental news, or sector out of favor
3. **Quality:** Positive earnings trajectory, accelerating growth

## Exit Triggers

- P/E/G > 1.5: fully valued, exit
- Revenue growth decelerates: thesis warning
- Aged > 10 days: review
- Better GARP opportunity found

## Sentiment Overlay

| Sentiment | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Bullish consensus + stock cheap | Skeptical — verify |
| Bearish consensus + stock cheap | **BUY signal** (contrarian) |
| Bullish consensus + stock expensive | SELL |
| Bearish consensus + stock expensive | HOLD (wait for better entry) |

## Sizing

Base = 2% of portfolio.
- Contrarian buy (bearish consensus): 1.5x
- All 3 criteria met: 1.0x
- Only 2/3 criteria: 0.5x probe
