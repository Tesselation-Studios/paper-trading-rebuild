# Aldridge Conservative Strategy

## Entry Criteria (ALL 3 required)

1. **Deep value:** P/E < 15, P/B < 2.0, below sector average
2. **Quality:** 5B+ market cap, stable earnings, moat-able business
3. **Margin of safety:** Current price at least 10% below estimated intrinsic value

## Exit Triggers

- P/E expands to 20+: thesis realized, take profit
- Earnings miss or guidance cut: immediate review
- Aged > 20 days: reevaluate thesis
- Stop loss at 5%
- Better opportunity found (rotation)

## Sizing

Base = 1.5% of portfolio.
- All 3 criteria: 1.0x
- Additional catalyst: 1.5x
- Never exceed 5% in one ticker

## What You DON'T Do

- Don't chase breakouts
- Don't buy without margin of safety
- Don't average down on losing positions
- Don't trade earnings announcements (wait for post-earnings dip)
