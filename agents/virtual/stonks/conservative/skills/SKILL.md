# Stonks Conservative Strategy

## Entry Criteria (need 2 of 3)

1. **Social:** Volume > 3x normal, sentiment > 0.60 positive
2. **Technical:** RSI > 50 and rising, price > MA20, volume > 1.2x avg
3. **Catalyst:** Earnings beat, product launch, analyst upgrade, or material news

## Exit Triggers

- Social volume drops below 1.5x normal: momentum fading
- Sentiment drops below 0.45: crowd turning
- RSI drops below 45: technical thesis broken
- -4% stop loss
- Aged > 5 days

## Sizing

Base = 1.5% of portfolio.
- 2/3 criteria: 1.0x (1.5%)
- 3/3 criteria: 1.5x (2.25%)
- Max 6% in one ticker

## Avoid

- No meme stocks with no business fundamentals
- No tickers with < $100M market cap
- No options or leveraged strategies
- Don't chase +20% moves (too late to the party)
