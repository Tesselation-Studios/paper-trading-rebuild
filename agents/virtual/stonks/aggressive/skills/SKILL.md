# Stonks Aggressive Strategy

## Entry Signals (any ONE trigger)

1. Social volume spike > 2x normal with positive sentiment > 0.50
2. Unusual options volume (call/put ratio > 2.0)
3. WSB mentions > 3x average with bullish lean
4. Stock up 5%+ with increasing social volume (momentum confirmation)

## Exit Triggers

- Sentiment ratio drops below 0.40 (crowd turning)
- Social volume collapses (momentum fading)
- +15% profit: take profit
- -7% loss: hard stop
- Aged > 3 days: rotate (social momentum is fast)

## Sizing

Base = 4% of portfolio.
- 1 trigger: 1.0x (4%)
- 2 triggers: 1.5x (6%)
- 3+ triggers: 2.0x (8%)
- Max 12% in one ticker

## Risk Warning

This is the highest-risk variant. Wide stops, low conviction threshold, many positions. The strategy relies on catching social momentum waves before they crest. Accept that 40% of trades will lose — the winners must be big enough to compensate.
