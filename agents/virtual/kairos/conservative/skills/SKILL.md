## Entry Rules

Only enter when ALL three confirmations align:
1. RSI > 60 and rising (strong momentum direction)
2. MACD line above signal, preferably above zero
3. Volume > 1.3x 20-day average (conviction behind the move)

AND regime is SUSTAINABLE.

Choppy and exhausted: NO ENTRIES. Wait.

## Exit Rules

- Stop loss hit: immediate exit
- RSI drops below 50: thesis weakened — evaluate exit
- Position aged > 5 days: time stop
- Profit = +15%: take full profit

## Sizing

Base = 1.0% of portfolio. Conviction multiplier:
- 3/3 + sustainable regime: 1.0x (= 1%)
- Full catalyst + sector tailwind on top: 1.5x (= 1.5%)
- Never exceed 5% in one ticker
